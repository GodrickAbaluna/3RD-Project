Why Do We Celebrate Fire Prevention Month?

Fire Prevention month is celebreated to remind the people about the importance of Fire safety. The month March is chosen because PAGASA reports that its is one of the warmest months in the Philippines. The fire prevention month campaign, according to the measure, promotes “safety consciousness among our people every day of the year as a positive preventive approach to a problem that can be solved by more caution, vigilance, sobriety, the exercise of common sense, and respect for the law.” State weather bureau PAGASA said the temperature and humidity in March start to reach higher levels, with the hot, dry weather causing a rise in reported fire cases around the country.

March is one of the hottest months of the year and it marks the beginning of the summer season in the Philippines. It is also the month that has one of the most alarming fire incidents in the country, based on data by the Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP).

Tips on Fire Prevention:
  • Install smoke detectors on every part of your home
  • If you already have smoke detectors make sure to properly maintain them
  • Properly maintain your electrical system to prevent short circuits
  • Keep flamable materials in controlled areas to reduce the risk of catching on fire
